"The Evil Within"

Enter the abyss of horror...

Overview

"The Evil Within" is a survival horror game developed by Tango Gameworks and published by Bethesda Softworks. Set in a nightmarish world where sanity is tested, players take on the role of Detective Sebastian Castellanos as he explores a gruesome and twisted world. The game combines intense survival horror elements with a gripping story and disturbing imagery.

Features

  • Terrifying atmosphere and disturbing environments
  • Engaging and psychological horror gameplay
  • Varied and nightmarish enemies
  • Resource management and survival tactics
  • Intricate and unsettling storyline

Cast & Crew

  • Director: Shinji Mikami
  • Producer: Masato Kimura
  • Voice Actors:
    • Doug Bradley as Ruvik
    • Roger Clark as Detective Sebastian Castellanos
    • Jennifer Carpenter as Julie Kidman
    • Yuri Lowenthal as Joseph Oda

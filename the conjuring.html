The Conjuring - Movie Details

"The Conjuring" is a 2013 supernatural horror film that has become a modern classic, known for its intense scares and gripping narrative. Here are some key details about the film:

  • Director: James Wan
  • Starring: Patrick Wilson, Vera Farmiga, Ron Livingston, Lili Taylor
  • Genre: Horror, Supernatural
  • Duration: 112 minutes
  • Rating: R

Discover the Horror of "The Boy"

When you break the rules, prepare for the consequences...

"The Boy" is a chilling tale of a young woman hired to care for an English couple's life-sized doll, which they treat as their son. However, not everything is as it seems, and the doll may have a life of its own...

The Boy - Movie Details

"The Boy" is a 2016 horror-thriller that keeps audiences on edge with its eerie atmosphere and unexpected twists. Below are some details about the film:

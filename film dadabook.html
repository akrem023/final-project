Uncover the Horror of "The Babadook"

When fear manifests, there is no escape...

"The Babadook" is a psychological horror film that tells the story of a mother and her son who are haunted by a sinister presence after discovering a mysterious book. As the darkness envelops their lives, they must confront their deepest fears to survive.

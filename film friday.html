"Friday the 13th"

Welcome to Camp Crystal Lake...

Synopsis

In the seemingly peaceful and secluded Camp Crystal Lake, terror strikes when a masked killer begins hunting down the camp counselors one by one. As night falls and the legend of Jason Voorhees comes to life, the camp's dark past is revealed, and the survivors must fight to see the light of day. Will anyone escape the curse of Friday the 13th?

Alan Wake 2

About the Game

*Alan Wake 2* is a psychological horror game developed by Remedy Entertainment. It continues the story of Alan Wake, a writer who finds himself trapped in a nightmarish world where reality and fiction blur.

Release Information

*Alan Wake 2* is set to release on October 17, 2024, for PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X/S, and PC.

Features

  • Intense psychological horror and suspense.
  • Enhanced visuals and immersive gameplay mechanics.
  • Deep, engaging narrative that expands on the original game.

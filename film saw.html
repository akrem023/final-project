Experience the Terror of "Saw"

When the game begins, survival is the only option...

"Saw" is a gripping horror film that follows two men who find themselves trapped in a dark, grimy bathroom with a deadly game laid out by the infamous Jigsaw killer. As they struggle to escape, they must confront their own past and make harrowing choices...

Saw - Movie Details

"Saw" is a 2004 horror-thriller that introduced audiences to the twisted games of Jigsaw. Here are some details about the film:

  • Release Date: October 29, 2004
  • Director: James Wan
  • Starring: Cary Elwes, Danny Glover, Leigh Whannell
  • Genre: Horror, Thriller
  • Duration: 103 minutes
  • Rating: R

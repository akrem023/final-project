Silent Hill

Enter the darkness where fear takes shape...

Synopsis

"Silent Hill" delves into the eerie and unsettling world of the small town of Silent Hill, where foggy streets and haunting sounds conceal nightmarish creatures and a dark past. The game follows the protagonist as they search for their missing loved one, unraveling the town's terrifying secrets along the way.

Cast & Crew

  • Voice Actor: David Schaufele as Harry Mason
  • Voice Actor: Donna Burke as Dahlia Gillespie
  • Composer: Akira Yamaoka

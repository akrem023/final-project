"Wrong Turn"

Get lost in terror...

Synopsis

"Wrong Turn" is a chilling horror film that follows a group of friends who venture off the beaten path and find themselves stranded in a remote wilderness. They soon discover that they are being hunted by a family of cannibalistic mountain men who live in isolation. With their lives at stake, the friends must fight to survive and find a way to escape the nightmarish situation.

Cast & Crew

  • Eliza Dushku as Jessie
  • Desmond Harrington as Chris

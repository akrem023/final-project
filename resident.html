"Resident Evil Village"

Survive the horror in a desolate village...

Overview

"Resident Evil Village," also known as "Resident Evil 8," is the latest installment in the acclaimed Resident Evil series. Set in a mysterious and eerie village, the game follows Ethan Winters as he searches for his kidnapped daughter while facing terrifying new threats and monstrous creatures. With a blend of survival horror and action elements, it promises an intense and immersive experience.

Features

  • Stunning graphics and realistic environments
  • New gameplay mechanics and horror elements
  • Encounter a variety of terrifying enemies and bosses
  • Engage in a gripping storyline with multiple twists
  • Explore an expansive and atmospheric open-world village

Cast & Crew

  • Director: Morimasa Sato
  • Producer: Tsuyoshi Kanda
  • Voice Actors:
    • Glen Powell as Ethan Winters
    • Christine Nguyen as Mia Winters
    • Jeanette Maus as Donna Beneviento
    • Doug Stone as Heisenberg
